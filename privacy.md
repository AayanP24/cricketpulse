# Privacy Policy for CricketPulse

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Aayan Abhay Patil provides theCricketPulse app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. You can use our app without providing any personal details like your name, email, or phone number.

### 2. Third-Party Data
Our app fetches cricket statistics and scores from third-party APIs. These services do not receive any of your personal data from us.

### 3. Log Data
We do not store or transmit any log data or usage history to any external servers. All statistical analysis is performed locally on your device or via public data streams.

### 4. Children's Privacy
Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
